How It Protects You

Every design decision puts your privacy first.

🔒

No Public Feed

Nobody can browse all reports. There is no public list of lost or found items — completely eliminating fraudulent ownership claims.

🤖

Private AI Matching

AI silently scans reports in the background. Only the two matched users are notified — contact details stay hidden until both confirm.

💬

Session-Scoped Chat

Messages exist only within your private match session. Nobody else — not even admins — can read your conversation without cause.

✅

Dual Confirmation

Contact details and images are revealed ONLY after both parties confirm recovery — preventing identity theft and fraud.

⭐

Honest Rewards

Stars and credits are awarded only when the lost-item owner confirms recovery. Zero gaming, zero fake points.

📷

Auth-Gated Images

Uploaded images are served only to session participants. Attempting to access someone else's image returns a 403 Forbidden.
